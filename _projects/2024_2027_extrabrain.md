I am involving in this project as a part of KTH. This project is one of the source of my PhD funding. I am creating a hardware accelerator for Brain-Like computing with BCPNN learning algorithm in FPGA.

EXTRA-BRAIN is a 38-month EU-funded project under HORIZON Europe, focused on creating lightweight, energy-efficient, and data-efficient AI models. It explores advanced learning methods like transfer learning, one-shot learning, and lifelong learning to reduce input needs without sacrificing output quality. Learn more at [extra-brain.eu](https://www.extra-brain.eu/).

The project brings together 12 partners from 8 EU countries, led by KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm. Together, we're building smarter, more sustainable AI for the future.
